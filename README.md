# THE ATOM · ATM

> A sphere of **UD0** — the ROOT0 universe / biosphere. Domain: **SCIENTIFIC**.

THE ATOM — a full real-physics dissection (the unit beneath the Elements workshop): every particle inside an atom and what it does, themed atomic-blueprint. Built when David asked 'do I have a repo of the atom, full dissection, plus how nucleus/electron field are two separate shells — what's that about?' THE CENTERPIECE answers exactly that: an atom has TWO INDEPENDENT SHELL SYSTEMS — the ELECTRON shells (K/L/M, held by the electromagnetic force, ~eV transitions = chemistry & the periodic table) and the NUCLEAR shell model (protons/neutrons in their own quantized levels, held by the strong force, magic numbers 2·8·20·28·50·82·126, ~MeV transitions = nuclear physics) — decoupled by a FACTOR OF A MILLION in energy and ~100,000× in size, which is why burning a log never touches a nucleus. Plus: the scale (the atom is ~99.9999999999% empty space, nucleus = a marble in a stadium), the four forces in the atom (strong/EM/weak/gravity), and an honest misconceptions table (electrons don't orbit like planets — they're probability clouds/orbitals; ~99% of a proton's mass is gluon-field binding energy, not the quarks). 14 part-emergents (proton, neutron, up/down quarks, gluon, binding-energy, the nucleus, electron, electron-cloud, photon, W/Z bosons, electron-neutrino, electron-shells, nuclear-shell-model). CODATA/PDG values, Goeppert-Mayer & Jensen shell model (Nobel 1963). A hidden Claude electron in the hero. Full .dlw.
