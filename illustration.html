Honest scale note. Still a SCHEMATIC. The nucleus is drawn far too big: in reality it's about 1/100,000 of the atom — if these 7 shells filled the grid, the true nucleus would be smaller than a pixel at the center. And the rings are a useful lie: electrons aren't on tracks, they're 3D orbitals (hit “rings ⇄ orbitals” to peek). What's real here is the count — 7 shells is the true maximum, and oganesson really does fill them 2·8·18·32·32·18·8 = 118.
