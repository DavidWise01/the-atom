Honest note. The exact orbital math (En = −13.6 eV/n², standing waves, ⟨r⟩ ≈ n²a₀) is for hydrogen — multi-electron atoms like oganesson are close cousins, not exact. The “rings” are Bohr’s 1913 model; the “clouds” are Schrödinger’s 1926 truth. Both describe the same atom — the isomorphism is that Bohr’s integer shells are exactly where an integer number of de Broglie wavelengths fit. And the scale is still a schematic: the real nucleus is ~1/100,000 of the atom, smaller than a pixel here.
